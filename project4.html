Lucis Candies

This is a logo I designed in Adobe Illustrator for a client who wanted to start up their own small candy-selling business. I went with the client's requested colors: a light blue and yellow color palette. I added brown for the visual of the bear and a black border around the typography and the elements present in the logo for a clearer perspective and separation of elements used in the logo.

Tools used: Sketching + Adobe Illustrator + Adobe Photoshop
