Valle Landscape Construction

This logo was designed in Adobe Illustrator for Valle Landscape Construction, a Central Valley business. Green was chosen as the primary color to symbolize nature and reflect the company’s landscaping focus. The design features a tree on a grassy surface above the company name, with a flat landscape below, clearly representing the industry. Outlined elements form a shield, symbolizing protection, stability, and the owner's commitment to preserving the land.

The design process involved sketching + Adobe Illustrator, + Adobe Photoshop + Adobe XD.
